What lessons did you take from the first chapter of Search Inside Yourself?

I found it to be interesting and I am keen to learn more on the subect of Emotional Intelligence. I learned that mindfulness and awareness, of your emotions and others, can be harnessed for performing work, leadership and creating the conditions for happiness.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

It was very relaxing. I found it best not to worry if I started to think about something, as worrying I was "doing it wrong" would only make it worse. When that happended I would try to let whatever thought it was drift off and focus on my breathing.
